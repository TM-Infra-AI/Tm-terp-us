About Us

The Premier ERP Solution for the Tile business

Tile ERP (TERP) is a powerful, Salesforce-based affordable solution designed to modernize and streamline operations in the tile industry.

Backed by AI, TERP seamlessly integrates inventory management, order processing, and financial accounting, TERP enhances efficiency, transparency, and control.

Our all in one solution streamlines every aspect of your business, providing entire visibility to enhance efficiency and profitability.

Let’s give you a walkthrough of TERP

Key Features

Sales & Order Management

  • Backed with AI, seamlessly manage sales orders, quotations, and customer inquiries.
  • Automated pricing and discount management.
  • Integrated CRM for improved customer communication.
  • Real-time tracking of order status and delivery schedules.
  • Smooth process of Cancellation and refund process.

Inventory & Warehouse Management

  • AI-driven inventory optimization with real-time stock visibility.
  • Multi-warehouse and multi-location tracking enhanced by AI-based analytics.
  • Intelligent stock reordering using AI-powered demand forecasting.
  • Seamless management of Inventory and Accounting.

Procurement & Vendor Management

  • AI-optimized supplier management and purchase order processing.

Accounting & Finance

  • AI-enhanced financial management.
  • Financial reports and audit trails for real-time transparency.
  • Transparency in Journal, Transactions and Sales Invoicing.
  • Cash Matching to ensure amount settlement is correct without any mismatch in Year End Process.
  • ML-based fraud detection on check issuance (flag out-of-range or suspicious checks).
  • GenAI to explain or alert staff about flagged checks.
  • Account payable invoice generation.
  • Accounting Process Creation of Budget and Balances.
